## Notes

- **Camera Motion**: Tracking, dolly, crane, and handheld movements are not included 
- **Advanced Techniques**: Time-lapse, slow motion, and special effects shots excluded
- **Usage Tips**: Combine different shots and angles to create dynamic sequences

## Terminology Quick Reference

- **EWS/XWS**: Extreme Wide Shot / Extra Wide Shot
- **WS**: Wide Shot
- **MWS**: Medium Wide Shot
- **MS**: Medium Shot
- **MCU**: Medium Close-Up
- **CU**: Close-Up
- **ECU/XCU**: Extreme Close-Up
- **OTS**: Over-the-Shoulder
- **POV**: Point of View
